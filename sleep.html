Sleep

How Important is Sleep?

Sleep is a fairly simple concept with some large ramifications. There is some variation within the population but the vast majority of people require about 7-8.30 hours of sleep. Sleep is such a vital aspect of health that gets largely overlooked and not enough attention paid.♥

Benefits of Sleep

  • Improve mental health: Improved tolerance to stressful situations and better regulations of emotion
  • Improve autoimmune system: Cleaning and clearing out within the body happens significantly betterthrough different stages of sleep.
  • Improve weight management ability: Consistently getting a good nights sleep can help the body better regular weight, reduce cravings of easy to eat high calorie dense foods and curb appetite.
  • Higher energy levels
    • ♥

Tips for better sleep

  • Keeping the room a good temperature. Generally, slightly cooler is better for getting to sleep
  • Curb bright light usage before bed. Blue light doesn't seem to imapct this so a red light filter on your phone likely won't cut it. Try to power down an hour before bed.
  • Stick to a bedtime and a wake up time. Sleep consistency takes time but eventually allows for a better, more consistent sleep schedule.
