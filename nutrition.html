Nutrition

Considerations when thinking about nutrition

Nutrition is both easier and more complicated when people realise. A major problem with nutrition content stems from incorrect information, or information targeted at the elite athlete. Both of which are unhelpful for the vast majority of people.♥

Make it easy!

To really make it easy, the basis of obtaining a funnctional weight and a healthy lifestyle is to consider eating within either a maintanence calorie intake, or a calorie deficite. Notice a healthy weight, in addition to a healthy body fat percentage. Being a certain weight, shape or fat percenteage doesn't necessarily correlate to being healthy. Keep it simple by not overeating, and moving more than you sit. ♥

Some Considerations

  • Whole foods, not processed: Processed foods tend to be calorically higher, less nutritionally dense and less satiating (filling).
  • There are no bad foods: Try to recognise when you're eating too many processed or calorically high foods
  • You don't need to overhall your diet to be healthy: Try to change a daily snack to a healthier version
